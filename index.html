Nagpur is a major city in the Indian state of Maharashtra and is often called the "Orange City" due to its high production of oranges. It is the winter capital of Maharashtra and serves as a key political and economic hub. Nagpur is an emerging IT and industrial hub, with companies like Infosys and TCS setting up offices. It also has a strong presence in coal, minerals, and textile industries. The MIHAN (Multi-modal International Cargo Hub and Airport at Nagpur) is a key economic project boosting logistics and trade.

  • Location: Central India
  • State: Maharashtra
  • Population: Over 2.9 million (as per the latest estimates)
  • Language: Marathi (official), Hindi, and English widely spoken
